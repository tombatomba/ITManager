## Regular Obligations of the IT Department
- External audit in the last quarter
- Internal audit in Q3
- Monthly submission of reports to UOS
- Quarterly submission of claims reports to UOS
- Project reports every two weeks
- Weekly SOC meeting
- Weekly collegium every Tuesday
- Review of incoming invoices

## Absence Rules
- Employees cannot work from home on Mondays and Fridays
- Employees must enter absence requests at least 7 days before the start of leave
- Employees must ensure that their absence does not overlap with their designated backup colleague
- Employees must carry their smart card reader if it is required for their work

## Budget Rules
- Must include the name of the planned vendor
- All amounts are without VAT
- VAT amounts to 20% of the base price
- If quarterly payment differs by more than 50% from the previous quarter, raise an alarm
- All amounts are in EURO currency

## Task Rules
- Tasks must be limited to a maximum of 5 working days
- Tasks must have adequate feedback
- Task title must be a maximum of 140 characters
- Task date cannot be after the Activation date

## Communication Rules
- Swearing and insults are prohibited
- Communication must be short and substantive
